About

I’m Dr. Willem van der Maden, a researcher at the IT University of Copenhagen, exploring how people interact with AI to design systems that enhance wellbeing and improve user experiences.

My research focuses on developing frameworks and methodologies for designing AI systems that actively promote wellbeing and human flourishing. I’m particularly interested in how we can create AI technologies that not only avoid harm but actively contribute to individual and community wellbeing.

Through my work, I aim to bridge the gap between AI technology and human needs, ensuring that our advances in artificial intelligence are aligned with human values and promote positive outcomes for society.
