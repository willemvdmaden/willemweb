I'm thrilled to share that I successfully defended my PhD thesis on "Designing Positive AI" at TU Delft!

My research focused on developing frameworks and methodologies for designing AI systems that actively promote wellbeing and human flourishing. This journey has been an incredible exploration of how we can create AI technologies that not only avoid harm but actively contribute to individual and community wellbeing.

Thank you to my supervisors, committee members, colleagues, and family for their support throughout this journey. Looking forward to continuing this important work in my next chapter!
