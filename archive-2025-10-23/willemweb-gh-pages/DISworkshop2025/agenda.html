Workshop Agenda

Our one-day workshop will combine presentations, group discussions, and hands-on activities to explore and address the semantic and practical stopsigns in GenAI and design research.

Workshop Schedule

Below is the preliminary agenda for our workshop on 6th July 2025:

09:30 - 09:45 | Arrival & Coffee

• Welcome participants
• Morning refreshments

09:45 - 10:00 | Welcome & Brief Overview

• Introduction to workshop goals and approach
• Overview of Semantic Stopsigns concept

10:00 - 10:15 | Ice-breaker

• Interactive activity to help participants connect

10:15 - 10:45 | Keynote + Q&A

• Featured keynote presentation (speaker to be announced soon)
• Interactive Q&A session

10:45 - 11:15 | Coffee Break

• Networking and refreshments

11:15 - 12:15 | Presentations

• Participant presentations of submitted cases and research

12:15 - 12:45 | Group Activity 1

• Collaborative work in small groups

12:45 - 14:00 | Lunch

• Lunch break with informal discussions

14:00 - 14:15 | Post-lunch Activation

• Brief energizing activity

14:15 - 15:15 | Discussion

• Group dialogue and reflection on workshop topics

15:15 - 15:45 | Coffee Break

• Afternoon refreshments

15:45 - 16:45 | Group Activity 2

• Advanced collaborative work building on morning sessions

16:45 - 17:00 | Wrap-up & Close

• Summary of key insights
• Next steps and future collaborations

17:00 onwards | Informal Group Dinner

• Continued discussions and networking (optional)

Post-Workshop Plans

Following the workshop, we plan to:

  • Create a collective document synthesizing the workshop discussions and outcomes
  • Develop a research agenda for addressing stopsigns in GenAI and design research
  • Explore opportunities for a special issue or collaborative publication

Note: This agenda is subject to adjustment based on the number of accepted submissions and specific interests of participants.
